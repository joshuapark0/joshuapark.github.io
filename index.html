My Story

I'm a computer science student driven by curiosity and a strong interest in how technology shapes everyday experiences. Over time, what started as an interest in coding has grown into a deeper focus on building thoughtful, user-centered applications.

Design has played a key role in that journey. Whether I'm developing web or mobile applications, I enjoy thinking beyond functionality—considering how something feels to use, how intuitive it is, and how small design decisions can shape the overall experience. For me, the intersection of development and design is where the most meaningful work happens.

Through my coursework, projects, and hands-on experience, I've worked with a range of technologies including Python, Java, React, React Native, TypeScript, and SQL, along with other languages and frameworks. More importantly, I've learned how to adapt quickly, collaborate effectively, and approach problems with both technical and creative perspectives.

I'm always looking to grow—not just by learning new tools, but by building better, more intentional experiences. My goal is to continue developing solutions that are not only functional, but also intuitive, polished, and impactful.
